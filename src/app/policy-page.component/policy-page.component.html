Terms of Service and Policy

1. Description

This website is an online version of the classic Connect Four game. The game can be played for free and does not require registration. The website is free of ads.

2. Personal data access

To provide players with an online identity, this website invites users to sign in with a social media account. This popular feature is called social login. The social login feature enables us to request and obtain the public information in your social media profile, i.e. your name, profile picture and email address. The name and profile image serve to create your visual identity in the game context. When you sign out or or close the browser window, your data is removed from view. We also obtain the email address in your social media profile, however this information is completely hidden from view.

3. Data collection

We do not collect, process or store your personal data or any technical information such IP address or device characteristics. This website does not use Google Analytics to collect anonymous data about users. The website uses however third-party services that can track your internet activity. The details of this usage are provided in the next section.

4. Cookies and privacy

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website, in order to retrieve information about your interaction with the website. For example, the items saved in your shopping cart or login status. Connect Four uses cookies to customize the behaviour of the website and to provide the social login feature. Cookies can classified into first-party and third-party cookies.

First-party cookies:

When you accept our Policy, we need to know that you have done so, in order to never bother you again about the Policy. We use a persistent cookie to this end.

Third-party cookies:

Third-party cookies are cookies that are set by a website other than the one you are currently on. When you sign into our website using your social media account, the third-party service that provides this functionality may set a tracking cookie on your device. Tracking cookies enable a tracker service to record your presence across some of the websites that you visit. This is done for marketing purposes and to serve you targeted ads. We are constrained to accept third-party cookies as an inevitable requirement of the social login feature we want to support. If you are concerned about this practice you should probably avoid logging in with your social media account on Connect Four and on any other place.

5. Communication

We do not send any kind of email messages to our users.
